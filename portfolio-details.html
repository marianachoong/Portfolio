The task of this project was to redesign a nonprofit website and create a UI prototype presentation and case study.

1. Problem

Emancipet has been unable to expand the call center quickly enough to meet the growing needs of the business.

2. Solution

Offer online tools for scheduling and vet record access to lessen the volume of calls to the call center.

3. Impact

3 out of 5 people were able to complete their tasks fully online.
Call wait times were reduced by 50%.





Problem Statement

How might we improve Emancipet's website so that pet owners can schedule a visit ahead of time and access their pet records, while also bringing awareness to the donation section of the website.

Hypothesis

We believe redesigning the Emancipet website to include apatient portal and online appointment scheduler will achieve a more thorough and efficient vet appointment for both Emancipet and its users.
